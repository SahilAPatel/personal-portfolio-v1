Missing Volleyball

04/07/2020

About a year ago I started playing volleyball because two of my friends at my coop were on the Company league's team and they needed a subsitute. Before I knew it I was subbing in almost every week and eventually I got really into volleyball! I have always played basketball so I was not a stranger to being on the court and understanding positions, but it was very itneresting seeing how different the sport plays. At first glance you'd think oh you just set the ball and spike it, but in reality a lot of fundamnetals as well as timing go into making a good play. There is also blocking, quick attacks, faking blockers and so much more! So as Corona hit and everyone became home bound, I have really been itching to play some volleyball. One of the cool ways I am relieving this urge is by watching videos on moomoocowjonny's YouTube channel, particularly the UCSB Men's Volleyball GoPro series! The videos are essentially a first person POV of a UCBS professional volleyball player and you get to see how insane these players are!
